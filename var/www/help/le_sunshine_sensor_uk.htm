Sunshine sensor

The sunshine sensor is used to calibrate the images depending on the sunlight. This makes it possible to compare photos over time, despite variations in light during photo shoots. The sunshine sensor is attached on the upper part of the drone, facing the sky. During flights the sunshine sensor is powered by the multispectral sensor.

It is recommended to use a TS32GSDU3X SD card with Sequoia. Performances are not guaranteed for other SD cards. Check SD card compatibilities on www.parrot.com/entreprises/sequoia.
